Just a Textarea

What it does

A full-screen plain text editor. Paste rich text from anywhere — formatting is stripped automatically so you get clean text ready to use.

How to use

  • Paste into the editor — text is stripped and copied to your clipboard
  • After pasting, the editor auto-clears in 10 seconds so you're ready for the next paste
  • Click + for a fresh session, or pick a recent paste from the bar below
  • Click Copy to copy what's in the editor

Keyboard shortcuts

  • ⌘/Ctrl + C Copy (when the editor is focused)
  • ⌘/Ctrl + K Clear the editor
  • Esc Close this help panel

Privacy

Everything stays in your browser. Text is saved locally — nothing is sent to a server.
